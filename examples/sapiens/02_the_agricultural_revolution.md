## pragmatic

- **Meaning:** dealing with things sensibly and realistically
- **Synonyms:** practical, realistic, sensible
- **Context:** "The shift to farming was less a pragmatic choice and more a gradual trap that locked communities into harder labor."

## superfluous

- **Meaning:** unnecessary, especially through being more than enough
- **Synonyms:** excessive, redundant, surplus
- **Context:** "Hunter-gatherers had no need for superfluous possessions — mobility demanded traveling light."
- **Date Added:** 2025-01-20

## nostalgia

- **Meaning:** a sentimental longing for the past
- **Synonyms:** wistfulness, yearning, longing
- **Context:** "There is a certain nostalgia in imagining the forager lifestyle as freer and more fulfilling than our own."
- **Date Added:** 2025-01-21

## diligent

- **Meaning:** having or showing care and conscientiousness in one's work
- **Synonyms:** industrious, meticulous, assiduous
- **Context:** "Early farmers had to be diligent in tending their crops, a far cry from the varied routines of their ancestors."
- **Date Added:** 2025-01-21
